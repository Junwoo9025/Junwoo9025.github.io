Overview

Purpose

These days there are so many colthings out there, and people are unsure of what to buy due to their price, quality, and design. Have you had experience spending a lot of money just simply because of the logo in the clothing? Our company’s purpose is anyone can easily accessible to our clothing, don’t worrying about the money, can get the best satisfaction in design, quality, and price.

Audience

J street is mainly targeting for, for those who loves Street Fashion. Especially, their are many options on clothings, we are targeting for unique, comfortable design with good qualities and price. Tageting age is around 15-30 yearls old. Our main goal for audience is building a fandom, so anyone can easily know our brand through them. .
