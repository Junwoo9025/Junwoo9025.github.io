All ABOUT JSTREET

I have always been interested in fashion- especially street fashion. So, I decided to create a website that showcases all the current street-style trends and components in a sleek manner. Not only design, but quality of clothing is very important to us which is why all the items on our site are high-quality pieces that represent what Street Fashion is.

With J-street's success comes the opportunity to travel and spread the J-street vibe. The clothing is inspired by an international group of musicians, skaters, DJs and artists with similar tastes; trendsetters in South Korea, London, Tokyo and other areas of the world are eager to get aboard this movement inspired by a network of like-minded individuals. The brand is committed to producing relevant, good quality clothing at a reasonable price available only at very select stores worldwide in limited quantities. This is an entirely new concept at this time, which has since been used to varying degrees of success.
